PANCHSHIL TOWERS

Panchshil Towers is designed for modern living and finer lifestyles. Located in Kharadi, it is spread over 15 acres consisting of 9 towers with 34 storeys each, offering stunning views of Pune's urbanscape. Almost 70% of open area is landscaped. Imaginative aqua-centric contours dominate the surroundings, bringing nature closer. The plush podium and modern clubhouse host a limitless range of amenities making life urban, convenient and truly extraordinary.

Project Features

  • 15 acres, 9 towers, 34 floors
  • 3-bedroom & 4-bedroom apartments in typical and simplex
  • Trendy 3-level penthouses & limited 5-bedroom podium-level villas
  • Carpet area sizes from 2,070 – 5,260 sq. ft. (193 – 488 sq. mtr.) (approx.)
  • Apartments with VRF cooling by Samsung
  • All residences with exterior viewing decks
  • World-class clubhouse with pools, playground
  • Common areas with user-friendly features for physically challenged and elderly citizens
  • Nearly 70% of total open area landscaped. Water features, lawn, promenades and vehicle-free avenues
  • Close to EON Free Zone, World Trade Center Pune, Magarpatta City, Hadapsar, and Ranjangaon MIDC
  • 7 minutes from Kalyani Nagar & Koregaon Park
